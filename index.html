Llamas: The Most Majestic Animal On The Planet

Read on to learn about the mysteries and wonders of the llama.

Where They Are Found

Llamas are normally found in Peru. That doesn't mean that llamas aren't in other places. They also are in other regions of South America. Llamas are also found on llama farms around the world (where the temperature supports them).

Their Food

Llamas eat grass, hay, and grain on a daily basis. This is somewhat the same as a horse. As a treat, they love apples and carrots that are cut into pieces. The cost for a llama's food supply would be about $20 to $30 a month, which is quite reasonable. Quick fact: A llama that is being abused or threatened in any shape or form might spit at it's threat. Based on this information, we can conclude that llamas are herbivorous.
